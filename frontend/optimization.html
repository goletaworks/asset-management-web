Workflow Mode Comparison

Trip-First

Flow: Opt 1 → Opt 2 → Opt 3

Groups repairs into trips first, then assigns whole trips to years. Keeps trips together but may defer high-priority repairs if their trip doesn't fit in Year 1.

  • ✓ Better resource utilization
  • ✓ Fewer total trips
  • ⚠️ High-priority repairs may be deferred with their trip

Repair-First

Flow: Opt 1 → Opt 3 → Opt 2

Assigns individual repairs to years by priority score first, then groups into trips within each year. Maximizes early placement of high-priority work.

  • ✓ Highest-priority tasks are finished ASAP
  • ⚠️ May split logical trips across years

Repair-First (Dynamic)

Flow: Opt 1 (with deadlines) → Opt 3 (smart) → Opt 2

Intelligently assigns repairs considering user-defined deadlines, location consolidation opportunities, and priority scores. Set "Must Finish By" deadlines in Optimization 1 table.

  • ✓ Respects hard deadlines (errors if can't fit)
  • ✓ Consolidates trips when possible to save money
  • ✓ Prioritizes sooner deadlines over later ones
  • ⚠️ May defer no-deadline repairs for better location matches

Tip: Try all three modes and compare results to see which fits your planning needs better.
